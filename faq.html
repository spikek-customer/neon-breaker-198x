FAQ

About the Game

Is this game free to play?

Yes. Neon Breaker 198X is free to play. The game is supported by advertisements, which allow us to continue development and provide updates.

Does the game work offline?

Yes. The game can be played offline. However, advertisements and some optional features may not be available without an internet connection.

How many stages are available?

The game includes a large number of stages designed to provide long-term enjoyment. Additional stages may be added through future updates.

Gameplay and Data

Does the game save my progress?

Yes. Game progress and settings are saved locally on your device. Currently, no cloud synchronization is used.

Will my progress be lost if I uninstall the app?

Yes. Since data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will remove your saved progress.

Social Media Sharing

Can I share my score or progress on social media?

Yes. The game includes a sharing feature that allows you to post your score or progress to social media platforms such as X (formerly Twitter), Instagram, LINE, and others.

Does shared content include personal information?

No. Shared images or text only include game-related information such as scores or stage progress. No personal information is included automatically.

Advertisements

Why are advertisements displayed?

Advertisements help cover development and operational costs, allowing the game to remain free to play and to receive future updates. We appreciate your understanding.

Can I disable personalized ads?

Yes. You can limit or disable personalized advertising through your device’s privacy or ad settings, such as enabling “Limit Ad Tracking.”

Language and Settings

Which languages are supported?

The game supports multiple languages, including English, Japanese, and Traditional Chinese. You can change the language from the in-game settings menu.

I found a translation error. What should I do?

While we strive to provide accurate translations, minor differences or errors may occur. We appreciate your understanding.

Troubleshooting

The game is not working correctly.

Please try restarting the app or rebooting your device. If the issue persists, ensure that your device has sufficient available memory.

An error occurred or the app crashed.

This may be due to temporary device conditions. Restarting the app usually resolves the issue.

Other

Does this app require account registration?

No. This app does not require user registration and does not manage personal accounts.

How can I delete my data?

Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app will remove all saved data from your device.
